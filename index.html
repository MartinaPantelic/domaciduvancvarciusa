Cena i dostava

Sveži duvan čvarci. Možete ih preuzeti, ili Vam ih šaljemo direktno na kućnu adresu iz Austin-a TX! Možete naručiti u Inbox. Cena $28/pound + troškovi dostave (u zavisnosti od lokacije).

Price and delivery

Homemade duvan čvarci (tobacco-style pork rinds), a Serbian delicacy made by slow-cooking pork until it dissolves and resembles finely-chopped tobacco. Price: $28/pound + shipping costs (pendent on shipping location).
